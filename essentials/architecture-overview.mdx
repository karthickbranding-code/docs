# Architecture Overview

## A Sovereign AI Architecture Built for Industry

YodaEdge is designed as a distributed, edge-native AI operating system that enables organizations to deploy, orchestrate, secure, and govern artificial intelligence directly within their own infrastructure.

Unlike cloud-centric AI platforms, YodaEdge moves intelligence closer to data sources while maintaining enterprise control over security, compliance, and operational workflows.

<Note>
YodaEdge follows an Edge-First, Cloud-Optional architecture. Data remains under organizational control while AI executes where operational decisions occur.
</Note>

# Why This Architecture Matters

Traditional AI platforms move data to the cloud.

YodaEdge moves intelligence to the data.

This architectural decision delivers:

* Lower latency
* Higher security
* Greater resilience
* Regulatory compliance
* Reduced operational risk
* Complete data ownership

<Note>
YodaEdge is designed to become the foundational operating layer for Industrial AI, enabling organizations to adopt advanced intelligence without sacrificing trust, control, or security.
</Note>
